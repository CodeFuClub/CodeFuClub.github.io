CodeFu

CodeFu is a club for developers to get together and discuss interesting topics. The goal is the improvement of skills through exposure to different ideas, but the focus should be on having a good time.

If you're interested in starting a chapter, you should consider the following guidelines:

  • Have fun
  • Presentations can be as opinionated as you want
  • Any topic is fair game
  • More people show up if there's food
  • Have fun
